This is the documentation for Jibber, a Java NNTP server. Jibber was created to service lightly loaded, local access newsgroups. It is not intended for use with a full (or even partial) "real" newsfeed. The idea is to simplify a small organization's internal communication by allowing people to use conventional news readers to read from and post to internal discussion boards.
